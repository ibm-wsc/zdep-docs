#Provisioning the Jump Server

The jump server is a separate TechZone reservation from your z/OS instance. Provision it before your engagement — allow approximately 30 minutes for it to reach Ready state.

⚠️ Note: IBMers only need their regular Cisco AnyConnect to access the environment. External users need to download AnyConnect or connect through the jump server.

  1. Go to the z/OS Demo Catalog page in the same collection as the z/OS environments and click the Collection Resource button on the Jump Server tile.
  2. In the collection it takes you to, click on the Environments tab, and then the "ibmcloud-2 environment" button on the Windows Server 2025 IBM Cloud VPC tile.
  3. The Create a Request form will appear. Type in a name and description (can be anything) and click the blue Next button.
  4. Select a Purpose. The selection here affects the duration of the reservation. Pilot and Demo require a valid IBM Sales Cloud (ISC) Opportunity ID. Education and Test do not. Once selected, click Next.
  5. Select a region. Choose one geographically close to your location for lowest latency. Click Next.
  6. Select the desired date and time range. Allow for ~30 minutes for provisioning plus additional time before a high-stakes engagement. Click Next.
  7. On the Configuration page, nothing needs to be done. Click the blue Review button.
  8. On the Review & Submit page, scroll to the bottom, check the terms & conditions checkbox, and click Submit.
  9. A success confirmation will appear with your Request ID. Click Track my request to monitor it.
  10. The reservation will immediately move to Provisioning status. Ocassionally it will first be in Scheduled status to begin, but then should move to provisioning shortly thereafter.
  11. When the reservation is Ready (approximately 30 minutes), you will receive an email from noreply@techzone.ibm.com with subject "Ready: <your instance name> on IBM Technology Zone".
